Hello!

I’m Kaylie Nguyen (or Diep Nguyen), a User Experience & Interaction Design student at Drexel University. I work as a UI/UX designer or researcher in my projects - these are also my two areas of interest that I desire to dive in and learn more about.

What brought me to UI/UX design is how practical and beneficial it is in our daily lives. It’s a creative process that is not only to create eye-catching and visually appealing designs but also to solve people’s problems with digital products and bring them a better user experience. I love it when I know that what I’m designing can benefit people and make their lives easier. User-centered design approach acts as my guideline through every design and research project.

As a college student, I aim to learn and explore as much as I can. Trying different positions in design projects is one of my goals (including project manager and web developer), which teaches me to be open-minded and look at the design process from different perspectives, as well as collaborate effectively with other people in a team. Secondly, constantly learning and improving my skills will stand me in a good stead in this everchanging field.

Currently I’m working on a project to design a digital platform prototype assisting teenagers in mental health peer support. A related Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) research has been done over the summer, and now I’m conducting a UX research and brainstorming some ideas for the prototype.

Beside designing, I enjoy going on picnics, taking a walk, reading and cooking. Recently I’m taking up photography and learning French. Sometimes you may find me spending my whole day exploring different neighborhoods in Philly!
